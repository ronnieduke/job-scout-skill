## Tips for Tailoring

When creating a version for a specific role:

1. **Read the JD carefully.** Identify the top 3-5 things they care about.
2. **Reorder competencies** to lead with what matches the role.
3. **Adjust highlight selection.** Surface the bullets most relevant to this role. You don't need to show everything.
4. **Mirror their language.** If they say "revenue operations," don't say "marketing ops." Use their words.
5. **Tune the summary.** The executive summary does the most work. Rewrite it for each major role type.
6. **Don't lie.** Reframing is fine. Fabricating is not.
