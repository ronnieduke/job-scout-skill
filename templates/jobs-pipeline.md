Run every new role through these before investing time. Customize these filters to match your priorities.

| Filter | What to Look For | Dealbreaker? |

| **Remote** | Must match your location/remote preference | Yes/No |
| **Comp floor** | Minimum compensation threshold | Yes/No |
| **Level/seniority** | Does the title and scope match your experience? | Preference |
| **Role type** | Builder vs. operator, IC vs. manager, etc. | Preference |
| **Company stage** | Startup, growth, public? Funding health? | Preference |
| **Tech stack fit** | Do the tools and technologies align with your skills? | Preference |
